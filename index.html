About Stella Obasanjo Hospital

Founded in 2007, Stella Obasanjo Hospital (SOH) stands as a premier healthcare institution, initially established to serve women and children with specialized services in obstetrics, gynecology, pediatrics, and family medicine. Named in honor of Nigeria's former First Lady, Chief (Mrs.) Stella Obasanjo, the hospital has evolved to meet the community's growing healthcare needs.

During the COVID-19 pandemic, SOH played a crucial role as a 300-bed isolation center, working alongside Federal, State, and private institutions to deliver essential care.

Under Governor Godwin Obaseki’s leadership, SOH has transformed into a five-star facility with a 250-bed in-patient center, outpatient services, and a new administrative block. Equipped with advanced infrastructure like an oxygen-generating plant and a 24-room doctor’s residence, SOH offers specialized services in radiology, emergency care, surgery, neurology, pulmonology, and a dedicated IVF clinic. Unique services, including a sleep clinic, physiotherapy, and prosthetics, further position SOH as a leader in specialized healthcare.

Edo State’s Vision for Healthcare

Edo State, led by Governor Godwin Obaseki, launched the Edo State Health Improvement Program (EDOHIP) in 2019 to strengthen healthcare infrastructure, empower workers, and improve access to quality healthcare. Partnering with Lauren Parker Limited, the program focuses on sustainable reforms, policy updates, and health insurance to build a resilient system that supports public health and economic growth.

The Vision

Governor Godwin Obaseki’s vision for Edo State prioritizes accessible, high-quality healthcare as a foundation for economic growth. In 2019, he launched the Edo State Health Improvement Program (EDOHIP), a comprehensive reform to strengthen healthcare infrastructure, expand access, and ensure sustainability. EDOHIP focuses on upgrading primary and secondary care, implementing health insurance, securing innovative funding, and enhancing healthcare education to build a skilled workforce. Governor Obaseki’s commitment ensures Edo State’s healthcare system is resilient, equipped to face future challenges, and positioned as a leader in regional healthcare excellence.
